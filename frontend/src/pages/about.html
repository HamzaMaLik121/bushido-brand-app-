THE RONIN CODE

BUSHIDO was forged in the neon-lit shadows of Neo-Tokyo. We are the architects of the masterless generation.

Our philosophy is simple: craftsmanship, discipline, and honor. We don't follow trends. We set the coordinates for those who walk alone. Every drop is a limited deployment, numbered and never to be seen again.
